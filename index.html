Systems & Cognitive Neuroscience

Visual representations in prefrontal cortex

I study how neuronal populations in vlPFC encode visual information and task variables. I combine multi‑unit electrophysiology with decoding, representational geometry, and computational models of working memory and dopamine‑based learning.

Current projects

  • Dynamics of visual representations in vlPFC — early vs late phases, low‑SF bias, and downstream readout.
  • Cross‑task generalization — choice vs cue color orthogonalization; decoding across WM and saccade tasks.
  • Perception ↔ working memory transition — non‑categorisable stimuli to map the time‑course of discrimination limits.
